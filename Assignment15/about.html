About Zoo Flies

At Zoo Flies, we specialize in creating custom hand-tied flies for all your fly fishing needs. Founded by Jack Kostomay, Zoo Flies offers a wide variety of flies that are perfect for both beginners and seasoned anglers alike.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality flies that help you catch more fish. We carefully select the best materials and techniques to create flies that perform well in different fishing conditions.

Why Choose Zoo Flies?

  • Custom-designed flies tailored to your needs
  • Expertly tied using top-quality materials
  • Perfect for various fishing environments and species
  • Affordable pricing and fast shipping

Meet the Founder

Jack Kostomay, the founder of Zoo Flies, is an avid fly fisherman living in Missoula, Montana who believes in the importance of crafting flies that not only look great but also catch fish.
