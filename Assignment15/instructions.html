Instructions for an Elk Hair Caddis

Materials

  • Size 14 Umpqua XT050 Hook
  • 12/0 Olive Semperfli Nano-Silk
  • Small Brassy Wire
  • Hareline Pheasant Tail Blend Dubbing
  • Cow Elk Hair

Instructions

Step 1

Insert Hook into vise

Step 2

Start a thread base

Step 3

Tie in small brassy wire with even thread base

Step 4

Wrap dubbing back towards hook eye

Step 5

Tie in hackle feather

Step 6

Wrap hackle feather back towards butt of fly

Final Step

Whip finish and apply head cement to complete the fly

Pro Tips for Beginners

- Keep your thread wraps even to maintain balance.
- Avoid cutting the elk hair too short; longer fibers help create a better wing.
- Practice dubbing with a small amount of material to avoid bulk.
