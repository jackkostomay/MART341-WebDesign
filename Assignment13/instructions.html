Instructions for an Elk Hair Caddis

Materials

Size 14 Umpqua XT050 Hook
12/0 Olive Semperfli Nano-Silk
Small Brassy Wire
Hareline Pheasant Tail Blend Dubbing
Cow Elk Hair

Instructions

Step 1

Insert Hook into vise

Step 2

Start a thread base

Step 3

Tie in small brassy wire with even thread base

Step 4

Wrap dubbing back towards hook eye

Step 5

Tie in hackle feather

Step 6

Wrap hackle feather back towards butt of fly

Step 7

Counter wrap wire over the hackle feather towards hook eye

Step 8

Tie off the wire

Step 9

Sweep hackle fibers back and tie in dubbing ball

Step 10

Tie in elk hair

Step 11

Whip finish and head cement
